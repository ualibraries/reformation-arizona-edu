True Concord Voices & Orchestra

Tucson's Grammy-nominated ensemble, True Concord Voices & Orchestra, derives its name from the Shakespeare’s Sonnet No. 8 about music, where he speaks of “. . . the true concord of well-tuned sounds . . .” True Concord is more than a name. It is a concept, a place and an experience, representing the power of shared emotions during concerts.
http://www.trueconcord.org/

Don’t miss any of the special exhibits, lectures, concerts and workshops being planned to commemorate the 500th anniversary of the Protestant Reformation in the Tucson community. We’ll add new events as they are scheduled, so check back often!
